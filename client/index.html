How it works..?

This is a small demonstration of how a Machine Learning works..here we will be using grayscale images as inputs

Prepare your datasets

  • You will first need to prepare your datasets, by adding images (28 * 28 pixels), with a label name

  • After clicking the "upload" button, this images will get saved to the backend with the label name as their folder name

Training the model

  • After the datasets are ready, you can start to train the model (minimun 2 datasets are required for training..)

  • Provide the no of epochs (or rounds) you want to train the model (value must be between 4 - 150)

  • The training history will be shown on the "Training history" box

  • After the training is finished, the model will be saved in your "client/model" directory

Making predictions

  • After the training is over and the model is saved, you can start making predictions and see the results

  • You can either choose an grayscale image file or draw on a canvas
